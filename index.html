About

I first dabbled in coding during the hay days of MySpace styling my profile with HTML and CSS. In undergrad at the University of Denver, I learned about brand and digital marketing and creating web design and web content in various internships and leadership positions. In my professional career, I’m an enthusiastic learner and excited to take on new challenges and opportunities.

After completing a leadership development program, I was motivated to pursue my passion for innovation and technology and formally learned full stack web development in an intensive and collaborative coding boot camp. The work I’ve accomplished speaks to my willingness to work hard and learn quickly. I look forward to being part of a team in which I can expand upon my skills and talent in web development and my passion for tech, learning, and helping others.

Outside of work and coding, I enjoy binging on the latest podcasts or HBO series, enjoying the Colorado sunshine, and planning my next adventure: next stop, Peru 2020!
